Forest

Switzerland's forests, which cover approximately one-third of the country's land area, are a vital part of its stunning landscapes, offering rich biodiversity and serving as essential habitats for a wide variety of flora and fauna.

These forests play a crucial role in environmental sustainability, providing habitats for a rich variety of wildlife and contributing to the country's efforts in biodiversity conservation. Additionally, attracting nature enthusiasts from around the world.
